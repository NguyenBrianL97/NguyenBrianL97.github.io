What are you? What do you do?

I am a recent University of California, Davis graduate. I graduated with a degree in computer science, but also spent a lot of my time doing engineering work. I guess that means you can call me a software engineer aspirant. I am looking for a company that I can work alongside to dump my passion and time into.

I was also on the UC Davis rowing team for four years. I trained six days a week in the mornings as early as 5 A.M. It was fun challenging different collegiate teams on race days, but now it is just as fun watching the program that was a huge part of my life grow, now as a UC Davis rowing alumnus.

Who are you? Who are you as a person?

I am a hardworking person that gets along with anyone. I am no stranger to people or challenges. Whether it be an indepedent task or a team goal, I put in the work to get the job done. I like being organized, dissecting a project into smaller organized parts makes everything more manageable and easier for the team or me to complete our objective.

I think life is best lived while both achieving your hardests goals and laughing. I always thought that perspective played a huge part in anything. Some people might view waking up at 5 A.M. for rowing practice as too early and ridiculous, but I saw it as one of the most memorable things in my life. Some people might view coding as frustrating and I do too of course, but the feeling at the end of a project is so rewarding that it makes it all worth it. In life, we are often thrown things that we cannot control so I am a firm believer that the best we can do in response is control how we feel about them.
